Welcome to my corner of the internet.

## The Beginning

Every journey starts with a single step, and this is mine. I've created this space to share my thoughts, learnings, and experiments in the world of software development.

## What to Expect

This blog will be a collection of:

- **Technical deep dives** - Exploring concepts, frameworks, and tools that catch my interest
- **Project showcases** - Sharing what I'm building and the lessons learned along the way
- **Random musings** - Thoughts on software, technology, and the craft of programming

## The Stack

This blog is built with modern, minimal tools:

- **Astro** - A fast, content-focused web framework that lets me write in Markdown (and MDX!)
- **Tailwind CSS** - For clean, utility-first styling
- **GitHub Pages** - Simple, reliable hosting
- **Giscus** - GitHub Discussions-powered comments, because why reinvent the wheel?

The design is intentionally minimal - cream background, monospace fonts, and a focus on readability. No unnecessary JavaScript, no bloat, just content.

## Why "Let there be light"?

It's a nod to beginnings - to the act of creating something from nothing, of bringing ideas into existence. Plus, it felt appropriate for the first post on a new blog.
